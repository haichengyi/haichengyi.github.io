Biography

I am pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences from 2017 to 2022 (passed PhD defense on May 20, 2022), under the supervision of Prof. Zhuhong You. From 2021 to 2022, I am a visiting scholar at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore, supervised by Prof. Kwoh Chee Keong. Before that, I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from the Henan University of Technology in 2017.

From Big Data to Big Mind, my reserch focuses on building data-driven, advanced artificial intelligence frameworks for solving cutting-edge issues in biomedicine and healthcare systems, where I have contributed more than 30 papers in journals and conferences like IEEE Trans., Brief. Bioinform., iScience, and IEEE BIBM. They have been cited more than 580 times (Google Scholar). Some of the work was reported by the offical website of Chinese Academy of Sciences.
